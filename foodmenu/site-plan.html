Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide accurate information about food trucks such as location, business hours, and menu. Customers will be able to find their favorites food truck easily because a picture of the food track and its location is shown on this website. Finally, this website shows what kind of food is offered for each food truck.

Audience

This website will be useful for families, students, and people between the ages of 16 and 65 years. People who like to support local businesses or like the experience to taste different kinds of food. In the same way, part of the audience of this website would be people who have a favorite food truck and they would like to eat there frequently.

Content

Home page

Food menu is the best place to find all information that you need about food served by food trucks. Finding information of food trucks in different websites or applications make difficult to choose a place to eat. However, our website "Find Food" make easy to find the location, food, prices, and the hours from most of the food trucks in Utah. If you want to get the latest infomation of your favorite food trucks or if you like to experiment eating new kind of food, "Food menu" is the website that you need to use.


Trucks

We offer one of the largest lists of food trucks in Utah that helps people to have a variety of options to eat and find their favorite food truck easily. You can find a list of food trucks ordered by name, location, and type of food that make easy search and find food trucks. Also, You will have the exact location of the food truck and you can use any GPS App to get the right directions to get to your favorite food truck. Because food is the essence of food trucks, you will see a brief description of the dishes why each food trucks are well-known, and what type of food they offer.

Images for the Trucks Page

Food

We unified the menu of thousands of food trucks in Utah to offer people a variety of options in one place to search and find food. A detailed description of each dish is provided to help people to find what they want to eat. You will see a picture, price, and ingredients of each dish that is helpful if you are looking for food according to a specific budget or if you are intolerant to specific food. With all the information provided by "Food Menu" we will help you to have an incredible experience eating food at food trucks.
